# Chapter 1 Assessment: NVIDIA Isaac and the AI-Robot Brain

## Assessment Questions

### Question 1
What are the main components of the NVIDIA Isaac platform?
- A) Isaac Sim and Isaac ROS only
- B) Isaac Sim, Isaac ROS, and Isaac Lab
- C) Isaac Sim and Isaac Lab only
- D) Isaac ROS and Isaac Lab only

**Answer: B**

### Question 2
How do Isaac Sim and Isaac ROS complement each other?
- A) They serve the same purpose
- B) Isaac Sim provides simulation while Isaac ROS provides GPU-accelerated algorithms for real-world deployment
- C) Isaac ROS is for simulation and Isaac Sim is for real robots
- D) They are completely independent systems

**Answer: B**

### Question 3
Why are GPU-accelerated pipelines important for robotics applications?
- A) They reduce power consumption
- B) They enable real-time processing of perception, control, and AI tasks
- C) They eliminate the need for sensors
- D) They make robots cheaper

**Answer: B**

### Question 4
What challenges does simulation-to-reality transfer address?
- A) Making simulation more expensive
- B) Transferring policies learned in simulation to real-world robots
- C) Reducing the fidelity of simulations
- D) Eliminating the need for real robots

**Answer: B**

### Question 5
What is the role of NVIDIA Isaac in Physical AI?
- A) To manufacture robots only
- B) To provide hardware acceleration, simulation, and deployment tools for AI-powered robots
- C) To sell software licenses
- D) To replace human workers

**Answer: B**

### Question 6
Which of the following is NOT a component of the NVIDIA Isaac platform?
- A) Isaac Sim
- B) Isaac ROS
- C) Isaac Lab
- D) Isaac Hardware

**Answer: D**

### Question 7
What advantage does photorealistic simulation provide?
- A) Lower computational requirements
- B) High-fidelity physics and rendering for realistic testing
- C) Reduced need for AI
- D) Faster robot movement

**Answer: B**

### Question 8
What is the main purpose of Isaac Lab?
- A) To provide simulation environments
- B) To provide advanced tools for sim-to-real transfer learning
- C) To manufacture robots
- D) To sell NVIDIA hardware

**Answer: B**

### Question 9
What does the acronym CUDA stand for in the context of NVIDIA Isaac?
- A) Computer Unified Data Architecture
- B) Compute Unified Device Architecture
- C) Computer Universal Device Access
- D) Central Unified Device Architecture

**Answer: B**

### Question 10
What is domain randomization used for in Isaac Lab?
- A) To reduce computational requirements
- B) To train policies that are robust to domain shift between simulation and reality
- C) To make simulations more expensive
- D) To eliminate the need for sensors

**Answer: B**
